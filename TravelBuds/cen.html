Centro de Turismo

Manila Philippines

The Centro de Turismo Intramuros Museum is a dedicated cultural and historical hub located within the historic district of Intramuros, Manila. It serves as a gateway for visitors to delve deeper into the rich history, culture, and heritage of the Walled City and its pivotal role during the Spanish colonial period in the Philippines.
