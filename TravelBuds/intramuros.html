Intramuros

Manila Philippines

Intramuros, known as the "Walled City," is the historic core of Manila, Philippines, and a testament to the country's rich cultural and colonial heritage. Built in the late 16th century during the Spanish colonial period, Intramuros served as the political, religious, and military center of Spanish rule in the Philippines. Enclosed by massive stone walls, it houses several notable landmarks.
