Binondo

Manila Philippines

Binondo, located in the heart of Manila, is recognized as the oldest Chinatown in the world, founded in 1594 during the Spanish colonial period. It has become a vibrant hub of culture, commerce, and culinary delights, reflecting the rich history and deep-rooted Chinese-Filipino heritage. This bustling district is known for its narrow streets lined with shops, restaurants, and historical landmarks that tell the story of centuries-old trade and cultural exchanges.
