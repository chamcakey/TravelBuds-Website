Arroceros Park

Manila Philippines

Arroceros Forest Park, often called the "Last Lung of Manila," is a tranquil oasis nestled in the bustling heart of the city. Located near the Pasig River and within walking distance of key landmarks like the Metropolitan Theater and City Hall, this urban forest park offers a much-needed escape from the city's hectic pace.
