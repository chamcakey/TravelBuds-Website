Memorial Circle

Quezon City, Philippines

The Quezon Memorial Circle (commonly referred to as QC Circle) is a sprawling national park and shrine located in the heart of Quezon City, the largest city in Metro Manila, Philippines. This iconic landmark is a multifaceted space that serves as a historical, cultural, and recreational hub for locals and visitors alike.
